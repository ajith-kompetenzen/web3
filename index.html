Who We Are ?

Ashs is a full-service media and PR company specializing in crafting strategic, creative campaigns that elevate brands and engage target audiences. While we have a deep passion and expertise in movie marketing, we bring that same level of creativity and precision to all of our branding efforts across various industries. Our experienced team combines industry knowledge with innovative strategies to develop compelling messaging and visually captivating content that resonates with audiences. From traditional PR to cutting-edge digital marketing, we tailor our approach to the unique needs of each campaign. At Ashs, we are committed to driving growth and maximizing exposure for our clients—whether in the film industry or any branding initiative—helping them achieve lasting success

About Us
Our Story

At Ashs, our team has been an integral part of the movie industry for over 6 years, with a proven track record of working on hundreds of films. We’ve built a reputation as a trusted partner for both major studios and independent productions alike. Our journey began with content creation and has evolved to encompass every facet of movie marketing. From creative design to the development of official posters, promotional videos, and more, we specialize in bringing a film’s vision to life in a way that resonates with audiences.

At Ashs, we are passionate about delivering exceptional results. By blending creativity, innovation, and expertise, we ensure that every project stands out and that our clients’ films receive the attention and recognition they truly deserve.

Our Services
At ASHS, we offer a comprehensive suite of marketing services tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients. From crafting compelling digital campaigns to executing offline strategies, our expertise ensures your message reaches the right audience in the most impactful way.
1. Social Media Marketing

(Facebook, Twitter, & Instagram) We create and manage dynamic social media campaigns that captivate audiences, build brand loyalty, and drive engagement. From creative content to targeted ads, we ensure your presence stands out on every platform. .

2. YouTube & TV Channel Marketing

Leverage the power of visual media with our tailored strategies for YouTube and TV channels. We design campaigns that amplify your reach, enhance visibility, and deliver measurable results for your brand or project.

3. Content Creation

Content is king, and we excel in creating compelling stories, blogs, videos, and visuals that resonate with your audience. Whether it's for digital platforms or traditional media, our content drives engagement and leaves a lasting impact.

4. Poster Designing

Our talented designers craft eye-catching posters that communicate your message effectively. From movie promotions to event announcements, we deliver designs that demand attention and inspire action.

5. Video Editing

With precision and creativity, our team transforms raw footage into stunning visuals. Whether it’s promotional videos, trailers, or ads, we deliver high-quality edits that captivate and communicate.

6. Marketing Auditing

Unlock the full potential of your campaigns with our in-depth marketing audits. We analyze performance, identify gaps, and provide actionable insights to optimize your marketing strategies for maximum ROI.

7. Anti-Piracy & BookMyShow Management

Protect your intellectual property with our robust anti-piracy solutions. Additionally, we streamline your ticketing processes with BookMyShow integration, ensuring hassle-free access for your audience.

8. Database Marketing

Harness the power of data with targeted database marketing. We curate and leverage audience insights to deliver personalized campaigns that drive conversions and build lasting relationships.

9. Offline Marketing

From hoardings to KSRTC bus advertisements, we take your message to the streets. Our public interaction campaigns and creative offline strategies ensure your brand remains visible and memorable in every corner.

10. Events

Whether it’s a product launch, movie premiere, or brand activation, we design and execute events that leave a lasting impression. Our expertise ensures seamless planning and flawless execution every step of the way.

At ASHS, our goal is simple—bringing your vision to life through innovative, impactful, and results-driven marketing solutions. Let’s make your story unforgettable.
